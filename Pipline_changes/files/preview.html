From the Sira of the Prophet

The Hijra: The Great Journey to Madinah

When the Quraysh plotted to assassinate the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), Allah commanded him to leave Makkah. With his loyal friend Abu Bakr, he made a secret and dangerous journey to Madinah, marking the beginning of a new chapter for Islam.

Ages 8-12·8 min read
After thirteen years of preaching in Makkah, the Muslim community faced terrible persecution. Many had already fled to Madinah seeking safety. Now, the Quraysh leaders had decided they would no longer tolerate the Prophet himself. They plotted to kill him. But Allah had other plans...
